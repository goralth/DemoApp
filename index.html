Montgomery County Mobility Hub

Executive Summary

The goal of the Montgomery County Mobility Plan is to examine the County's transportation network holistically.

Serving as the final deliverable to H-GAC and the County, this Mobility Hub encompasses all underlying data, scenario analysis results, and final projects.

A Need-Based Approach

The plan's methodology relies on two intertwined prongs:

  • Vision-Guided: Incorporating public and stakeholder inputs.
  • Data-Driven: Leveraging quantitative data to analyze system performance.

Countywide Overview

Vision & Goals

The plan's objectives are defined and tracked across six core performance areas.

MobilityEfficiently move people and goods.
SafetyProtect all users by reducing fatalities and crash risk.
Social AccessSupport multi-modal access and well-being.
EnvironmentalProtect natural resources and air quality.
InfrastructureEnsure condition and resilience of assets.
Economic VitalitySupport productivity and freight movement.
